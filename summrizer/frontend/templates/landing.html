Frequently Asked Questions

Does CarryOn Summary use third-party LLMs?

No. All processing happens locally on your machine or chosen server. No data is sent to external AI services.

Can I control the summary length?

Yes. You can use auto-sizing for optimal length or manually set target sentence count (4-80 sentences).

Which websites support auto-detection?

ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot, Poe, Perplexity, and many other AI chat interfaces.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. All text processing happens locally. No data leaves your machine unless you choose to deploy on your own server.
